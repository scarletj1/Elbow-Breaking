How to Break Your Elbow

This handy tutorial will help you get your elbow as utterly obliterated as possible.

  1. Find one trampoline. This is not mandatory, but most people find it helpful.
  2. Imagine a line going straight through your waist, from left to right. Rotate 180 degrees around this axis.
  3. Make one of your arms taut. We recommend your non dominant arm, but it is up to you.
  4. Listen carefully for a crunchy crackle and pop. Bonus points if you dislocate it too.

Here are a few more tips to make sure everything goes as planned.

  • To avoid infection, which could cause death, make sure you have an untrained medical professional in close vicinity to your location.
  • A common problem our customers face is that they lose the nerve to go through with the plan, even if they know they want it to happen. A good way to prevent this from happening is to have a trusted friend (preferably with experience in martial arts, but anyone will do) nearby to break it for you.
